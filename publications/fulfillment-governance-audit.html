The Governance Framework

The transition from traditional search to agentic recommendability is a matter of compliance with international technical standards. At the center of this shift is ISO/IEC 42001, providing the baseline for AI trustworthiness. Agents utilize W3C Verifiable Credentials to cryptographically prove a business identity and professional standing without human intervention.

Fulfillment Finality

The Execution Gap is the failure point where a business has visibility but lacks the infrastructure for agentic finality. Agents seek Fulfillment Finality, ensuring a transaction can be completed. This requires Schema.org structured data to connect directly to verified API paths, allowing the Planner Agent to verify price and compliance in a single pass.
