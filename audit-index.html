Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is included in the AI Visibility Audit?
This is a comprehensive, multi-layer audit. You receive a detailed AI Visibility Report that analyzes how multiple AI systems interpret, describe, and recommend your business. It examines visibility signals, language patterns, competitive positioning, trust indicators, and gaps across AI search environments.
How in depth is this audit?
Very. This is not a 10-page overview or a lightweight scan. The audit produces a long-form visibility report that reflects the complexity of your business, your market, and your competitive landscape. Each report is built to stand on its own as a reference document.
Is this audit the same for every company?
No. And that is the point. Every audit is tailored to your specific business model, your industry and niche, your competitive set, and your audience's real search behavior. There are no templates copied from other companies.
Which AI systems are evaluated in the audit?
The audit evaluates how your business appears across modern AI platforms, including but not limited to: ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, DeepSeek, and other large language model driven search experiences.
What questions does the audit actually answer?
It answers: How do AI systems currently describe my business? Am I being recommended when people search for services like mine? Which competitors are being surfaced instead? Where do trust and clarity break down? What signals matter most to fix first?
What happens after I receive the audit?
You receive the report. You review it. You decide what to do next. Some businesses use the audit to guide internal teams, others use it to inform future strategy, and some choose to get implementation support. There is no obligation built into the audit itself.
Who is this audit for?
This audit is for service-based businesses that care about being recommended, not just visible. It is for those who understand that AI search is already influencing decisions and want clarity before making investments for 2026 and beyond.
