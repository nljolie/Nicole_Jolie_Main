The Cooling-Off Rule does NOT apply to:

  • Business-to-business transactions

For complete details on when the FTC Cooling-Off Rule applies, visit: https://www.consumer.ftc.gov/articles/buyers-remorse-when-ftcs-cooling-rule-may-help
